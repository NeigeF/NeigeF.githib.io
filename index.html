About

I am currently an IMPRS fellow finishing my PhD in Astrophysics at the Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics in Heidelberg, where I study the Milky Way disk formation and evolution using forward modelling and statistical inference. I use high quality data from the APOGEE spectroscopic survey and the Gaia space mission to investigate the past history of the Milky Way disk, using it as a model organism for the formation and evolution of disk galaxies in a mor global context.
