About

I am a PhD student in Astrophysics at the Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics in Heidelberg, where I study the Milky Way disk formation and evolution through forward modelling. I use data from the APOGEE spectroscopic survey and Gaia to measure important properties of the Milky Way disk, such as the strength of radial mixing or the size evolution of the disk while it was forming.
